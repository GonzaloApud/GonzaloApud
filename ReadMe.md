# 💫 About Me:
I am a Computer Engineering student at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile (UC), currently in my fourth year of the Civil Engineering of Industries program.<br>My academic focus lies at the intersection of systems architecture and optimization, but my professional goal is to pivot into the Cybersecurity landscape of computer engineering.<br>Currently I'm mostly doing private projects for university, but I also do little side projects like web tools.<br>When I'm not debugging or optimizing hardware performance, you can find me deep in fantasy books, playing RPGs and ARPGs, or listening to early 2000s punk rock and nu-metal.
